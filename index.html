Processing

Proccesing is a java-based visual programming language that I chose as a topic for my junior year independent study. At about the same time I was learning the language, I was also beginning to draw, and decided that a project at the intersection of the two would be intresting. Here are three algorithims inspired by diffrent styles of art - each spawning hundreds of objects that act in diffrent ways to create the final image.
  • A re-creation of Pointillisim with Van Gogh's Starry Night.
  • Simulating painting with many individual brush strokes.
  • Mimicking crosshatching with Da Vinci's Mona Lisa.
